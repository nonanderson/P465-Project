Meet the Team

Committed to the ease and simplicity that rentals should be

Dev. Team: Nona Anderson, Tanner Hay, Adam Martinez, Connor Stacey

This website has been created because our team saw a problem with the rental process. Rather than having to deal with different realtors, the uncertainty of your staying time, or just general stressors surrounding finding a new environment to call your own, we are committed to empowering and motivating our cilents throughout that process. We want potential rental buyers to feel actually excited about a new home rather than dread the upcoming process of searching.
